Ben Swift leads the Cybernetic Studio. He's a researcher, artist, creative
technologist and educator whose work spans AI, computational art and music,
data visualisation, cybernetics and full-stack web development. As a
livecoding artist, he performs at international festivals including ISEA and
ICLC --- often as one half of the duo Funktion with Ushini Attanayake --- and
co-developed the Extempore programming environment for musical livecoding.
He's also a founding co-director of the ANU Laptop Ensemble, and received a
Vice Chancellor's Citation for Outstanding Contribution to Student Learning
in 2018. A common thread runs through all his work: "liveness" ---
human-in-the-loop interactivity with real-time feedback. Canberra born and
bred, Ben cares deeply about "making computing a welcoming, interesting and
creatively satisfying place, especially for people who don't fit the
traditional picture of a 'computer scientist'."
